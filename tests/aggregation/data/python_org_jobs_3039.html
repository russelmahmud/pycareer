PYTHON / OPEN SOURCE SOFTWARE / SCIENTIFIC COMPUTING ENGINEER New
Eomys Engineering Lille, 59 Nord, FRANCE

Job Title

PYTHON / OPEN SOURCE SOFTWARE / SCIENTIFIC COMPUTING ENGINEER

Job Description

As a member of our software development team, your main task will be to develop and maintain the new Python version of MANATEE software. Part of the work will consist in converting some of the current Matlab® code to the new Python oriented object architecture, but you may also develop new models (electrical, electromagnetic, structural, acoustic, …) to extend the software capabilities. These new modules will be developed from scratch (UML) together with the R&D engineers.

You will may also help the other R&D engineers on the internal research program of EOMYS (max 20% of the time) depending on your skills and motivation.

About the Company

EOMYS ENGINEERING is a company providing applied research services in electrical engineering, vibro-acoustics and heat transfer with more than 75% of its sales out of France. EOMYS outsourced R&D activities include multi-physics modelling, numerical simulation.
