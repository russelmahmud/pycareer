Python Developer New
Kitewheel Boston, MA, USA

Job Title

Python Developer

Job Description

Powering the next generation of marketing, Kitewheel is cloud-based software, used by the world’s largest digital agencies to deliver real-time customer experiences across social networks, web sites, mobile apps and more.

Qualified candidate will contribute to the development of back-end services for a large scale SaaS based customer engagement platform.

Essential Job Functions

  • Develops and integrates communications modules by incorporating 3rd party APIs into an existing analysis infrastructure
  • Integrates applications by designing interfaces to back-end systems such as database architecture and server scripting; studying and establishing connectivity with network systems, search engines, and information servers.
  • Recommends system solutions by comparing advantages and disadvantages of custom development and purchase alternatives

Restrictions

  • No telecommuting
  • No Agencies Please

Requirements

Essential Job Requirements

Education: Bachelor’s or Masters Degree in Computer Science or Computer Engineering or equivalent experience

Technology experience in the following areas is preferred:

  • 2-3 years of experience in back-end development using Python (2.7 or 3.4) in both threaded and multi-processing contexts. Flask or Django experience is a plus.
  • Knowledge of SQL and NoSQL databases and message queues.
  • Experience with deployment on AWS or Heroku, as well as tools for continuous integration.
  • Familiar with REST-compliant web service protocols and 3rd party APIs (Twitter, Instagram, Google Maps, etc).
  • Background in or knowledge of graph theory a strong plus

About the Company

Kitewheel is powering the real-time revolution for the world’s largest automotive, airline, retail, travel and consumer packaged goods brands. Four of the top five marketing agency groups use Kitewheel’s real-time Customer Engagement Hub to orchestrate individual consumer journeys, listening for “moments of truth” across Big Data and converting them into immediate, interactive, interconnected experiences. Kitewheel serves its global customer base from offices in Boston and London.

For more information please visit www.kitewheel.com and follow Kitewheel on Twitter and LinkedIn.

Kitewheel is an Equal Opportunity Employer

Contact Info

  • Contact: Jean Gaughan
  • E-mail contact: careers@kitewheel.com
